# Business Spec: Creatine + Magnesium Recovery Powder

**Date:** 2026-04-21
**Status:** Concept approved

---

## Overview

An online supplement business selling a custom **Creatine Monohydrate + Magnesium Glycinate powder** targeting active adults. Launches on Amazon FBA, expands to Shopify, with a long-term path to retail chain stores.

---

## Product

| Detail | Spec |
|---|---|
| Format | Stand-up pouch, powder |
| Serving size | 1 scoop per day |
| Formula | 5g Creatine Monohydrate + 300mg Magnesium Glycinate |
| Servings per pouch | 30 |
| Flavors | Unflavored (launch) — flavored variants later |
| Retail price | $39.99 |

**Positioning:** *"Two essential recovery supplements in one clean scoop"*
**Brand tone:** Minimalist, science-backed, clean label — no fillers, no artificial dyes. Inspired by brands like Momentous and AG1.

---

## Target Customer

- **Age:** 28–42
- **Gender:** Slightly male-skewed, but appeals to both
- **Household income:** $65k–$130k
- **Lifestyle:** Works out 3–5x/week (gym, CrossFit, running, cycling)
- **Supplement spend:** $80–$200/month already
- **Behavior:** Shops Amazon first, reads ingredient labels, values quality forms (glycinate vs. oxide)
- **Core pain point:** Already taking creatine and magnesium separately — wants simplicity without sacrificing quality

---

## Sourcing & Manufacturing

**Type:** Custom formula via GMP-certified US co-packer

**Recommended manufacturers:**

| Manufacturer | MOQ | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Makers Nutrition | 500 units | Full-service, beginner-friendly, handles labels |
| NutraScience Labs | 500 units | Strong support, fast turnaround |
| Supplement Manufacturing Partners | 250–500 units | Good for low-budget first runs |

**First run cost breakdown (500 units):**

| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| Ingredients (creatine + magnesium glycinate) | ~$1.50–2.50/unit |
| Manufacturing + pouch packaging | ~$2.00–3.50/unit |
| Label design (one-time, Fiverr/99designs) | ~$150–300 |
| **Total ~500 units** | **~$1,800–$2,500** |

**Lead time:** 8–12 weeks from formula approval to delivery

**Compliance notes:**
- Co-packer must be FDA-registered and GMP-certified
- Label requires Supplement Facts panel + FDA disclaimer
- Cannot make disease claims — use structure/function claims only (e.g., "supports muscle recovery")

## Unit Economics

**Selling price:** $39.99

| Item | Per Unit |
|---|---|
| Selling price | $39.99 |
| Amazon referral fee (15%) | -$6.00 |
| FBA fulfillment fee | -$4.00 |
| COGS | -$5.50 |
| Amazon PPC advertising | -$3.00 |
| **Net profit** | **~$21.50** |

**Break-even:** ~120 units sold
**Month 6 target:** 80–150 units/month = **$1,700–$3,200/month net**
**Shopify margin (later):** ~$27–28/unit net (no referral fee, pay for traffic instead)

## Sales Channel Strategy

| Phase | Channel | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Launch | Amazon FBA | Month 1–4 |
| Expand | Shopify (own store) | Month 4–5 |
| Specialty retail | GNC, Vitamin Shoppe, local health stores | Year 2–3 |

## Launch Plan

### Phase 1 — Build (Weeks 1–10)
| Task | Timeline | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Choose co-packer, finalize formula | Week 1–2 | Free |
| Hire label designer | Week 2–3 | $150–300 |
| Register Amazon Seller account | Week 1 | $39.99/mo |
| Purchase UPC barcode (GS1.org) | Week 2 | ~$30 |
| Product photography | Week 10 | $0–200 |
| Product in hand | Week 10–12 | — |

**Weekly time:** ~3–4 hours

### Phase 2 — Launch (Weeks 10–14)
- Ship inventory to Amazon FBA warehouse
- Publish listing: optimized title, bullet points, A+ content, keywords
- Enroll in **Amazon Vine** (~$200) for 10–30 early legitimate reviews
- Start PPC at $5–10/day targeting: *"creatine magnesium powder"*, *"magnesium glycinate creatine"*

**Weekly time:** ~2–3 hours

### Phase 3 — Grow (Months 4–6)
- Optimize listing based on converting keywords
- Scale PPC budget as ACOS drops below 25%
- Outreach to 5–10 micro-influencers (fitness, 5k–50k followers) for reviews
- Launch Shopify store for direct, higher-margin sales

**Weekly time:** ~2 hours

## Long-Term Vision

This business is designed to grow from a side income into a retail brand:

1. **Year 1:** Prove the product on Amazon, hit $2k+/month net
2. **Year 2:** Add Shopify, build email list, explore specialty retail
3. **Year 3–5:** Expand to national retail chains (Target, Walmart, Costco)

The online-first track record (reviews, sales velocity, brand identity) is exactly what retail buyers look for. Brands like Liquid IV and Momentous followed this exact path.

---

## Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Amazon competition | Differentiated formula + strong listing copy + reviews |
| Budget overrun | Start with 300-unit run to reduce risk |
| Regulatory issue | Use GMP co-packer who handles compliance |
| Slow early sales | Amazon Vine + PPC from day one |
| Quality issues | Request Certificate of Analysis (COA) from co-packer |
